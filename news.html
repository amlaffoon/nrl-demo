First Participants in our Pediatric Clinical and Administrative Mentorship

These mentorships are designed to equip participants with the knowledge, skills, administrative and practice tools necessary to operate a NeuroRecovery Program and to promote a recovery-based pedagogical approach to therapy.

The clinical program is set up to immerse the participant in the NeuroRecovery Network (NRN) clinic for 8 hours/day for a minimum of 1 and maximum of 4 weeks. The administrative program is designed to immerse the participant in the NeuroRecovery Network (NRN) clinic and administrative office for 8 hours/day for a minimum of 1.5 and maximum of 5 Days.

Learn how these mentorships programs can raise your understanding and approach to NeuroRecovery therapy • LEARN MORE
